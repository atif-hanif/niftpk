ISM Offerings

Image Archiving and Document Management System

Pakistan’s citizen data base is built on the images archived by ISM. In 2001, ISM completed the largest image archiving project in Pakistan, with 65 million national census document collected! Images, including thumbprints, were extracted from each document and placed in two-sided documents in multiple colors. Using special hardware/software compression technology to reduce image size during scanning, the project was completed in 9 months.

ISM has also archived over 50 million documents for two cellular companies and the largest refinery and insurance companies in Pakistan. Your data is safe in our hands.

Enterprise Content Management Services

ISM executes image archiving and document management. We oversee color printing of large volumes of fixed and variable data, inline perforation, mechanized insertion and web publishing.

Scanning Equipment

ISM offers high speed Simplex and Duplex scanners with speeds varying from 2000 to 8000 documents per hour.

Customer Care Support

ISM provides 24-hour customer care support for all imaging products and related hardware throughout Pakistan. We offer internal maintenance to NIFT on hardware including all brands for MICR Reader/Sorter equipment.
