Projects

Safeguarding your children’s education, your investments and your future, our systems move the Country forward.

BISE Automated Results Compilation Project – 2012 – Till Date Results Tabulation

NIFT has automated the results compilation process for all ‘9’ of the Boards of Intermediate and Secondary Education, Punjab. The project started in early 2012 and NIFT has successfully completed eight cycles of Results compilation for Intermediate as well Matriculation Annual and Supplementary Examinations.

Land Record MIS for Punjab Board of Revenue (LRMIS) – 2011 to 2016

NIFT was awarded two LOTs i.e. LOT1 (Districts Sialkot, Narowal, Gujrat & Mandi Bahauddin) and LOT2 (Districts Rawalpindi, Chakwal, Jhelum & Attock) for Scanning and Data entry of existing, paper based records in the shape of Registers to make Land Record Management Information System (LRMIS) operational and starting service delivery through this e-system.

Utility Payment Processing – 2001 till date

NIFT provides revenue automation services through scanning and data entry. We help process utility bill stubs for reconciliation and settlement for SNGPL, PTCL, K-Electric & KWSB.

Citizen’s Database (VeriSys); NADRA – 2000 to 2001

NIFT’s wholly owned subsidiary, ISM executed one of the largest image archiving projects in the world. ISM image-archived and tagged 61.58 million national census forms to establish the first citizen data warehouse in Pakistan by NADRA.

NADRA (BISP Project) – Oct 2009 to July 2011

NADRA awarded the contract of Double Data Entry services to NIFT for the Benazir Income Support Program of Government of Pakistan in October 2009. NIFT completed Data Entry of 0.71 million BISP forms. Each form entered contained more than 110 alpha numeric fields. A heads up data entry mechanism followed.

NADRA (Flood Affectees Project) – Nov 2010 to Jun 2011

NIFT has provided double data entry services for at least 300,000 Flood Affectee forms. NADRA complete the project under the umbrella of the National Disaster Relief Program (NDRP) of Pakistan.

NADRA (ECP Electoral Rolls Data Entry Project) – July 2011 – Feb 2012

NIFT has provided data entry services to undertake corrections of over 1.0 million Electoral Rolls forms. NADRA is doing the project for Election Commission of Pakistan. NIFT has provided indexing of over 50,000 forms on daily bases and the data entry of forms and registers in Urdu and Sindhi alphabets.

NADRA (ECP Corrected Voters List Scanning and Data Entry Project) – Feb to Mar 2013

NADRA had awarded the contract for Scanning and Data Entry of corrected voters lists of Karachi in first quarter of this year. NIFT had established ‘5’ scanning centres at District offices of ECP namely Karachi South, East, West, Central and Malir. Similarly, a data entry facility was established in Islamabad for extraction of data from the scanned forms.

EOBI Digitization Project – July 2012 to 2013

NIFT has started executing the digitization project for Employees’ Old-Age Benefits Institution (EOBI) in July 2012.

NADRA Electoral Rolls Printing Project – 2013

NIFT was tasked with printing over 22 million electoral rolls pages for the Election Commission of Pakistan during the 2013 National & Provincial elections.

Cellular Companies (Mobilink& Telenor Pakistan) – 2005 to 2009

ISM has completed image archiving/scanning and data entry of more than 21 million customer using service application forms of Mobilink. Similar activities have been done with over 6.23 million forms of Telenor during last 5 years, as a subcontractor to NCR Corporation.

Adamjee Insurance Company Limited – 2007 to 2008

Adamjee Insurance Company (Largest Insurance Company of Pakistan) awarded a digital archiving project of its Claims & Policies to ISM in June 2007.

National Refinery Limited – 2003 to 2004

National Refinery Limited, the largest refining complex of Pakistan, transformed its documents of its various departments onto digital/electronic medium along with the indexing of each file.

Certificate Authority/Public Key Infrastructure – 2004 ongoing

NIFT has designed and established Certificate Authority for issuance of digital signatures to authenticate and secure e-filing and electronic transactions. All facilitation is portal based and performed electronically.

FMAP PORTAL (KIBOR, KONIA, PKRV & SKUK) – since 2011

NIFT has designed and established web based portal for determining and disseminating live data for Financial Market Association of Pakistan (FMAP).
