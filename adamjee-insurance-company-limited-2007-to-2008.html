Adamjee Insurance Company Limited – 2007 to 2008

ISM has completed image archiving/scanning and data entry of more than 21 million customer using service application forms of Mobilink. Similar activities have been done with over 6.23 million forms of Telenor during last 5 years, as a subcontractor to NCR Corporation.

ISM has digitized more than 60000 claims comprising of around 0.89 million pages during eight months. Data entered along with images of Claims and Policies was integrated in the Adamjee intranet and is used by Adamjee Insurance for its online insurance claim activity.

Each claim file was indexed with approx. 40 characters comprising of Date, Claim number, Policy number Insurance Type etc. Project completed in 2008.
