Who We Are

Your finances flow seamlessly between banks every day, because NIFT looks after the processing and settlement. Welcome to the world of NIFT – National Institutional Facilitation Technologies (Pvt.) Limited. NIFT is the only Automated Cheque Clearing House and the prominent Payment System Operator in Pakistan.

Explore our range of services

NIFT eClearing

Automated Clearing services were introduced by NIFT in 1996, at a single center in Karachi. Growing organically, NIFT expanded services to all major cities in Pakistan, from Karachi to Islamabad and beyond.

NIFT ePay

NIFT ePay is providing an agnostic/adaptable payments platform – connecting all digital players through contextual digital payments. The payment gateway is accessible for all sizes of entities for convenient transactions via secure and interoperable ecosystem. All payments instruments and methods will be available in future to resolve the limitation of data driven payment digitalization.

NIFT eTrust

NIFT’s most extensive and technologically advanced venture, NIFTeTRUST ensures the safety of commerce and communications over the internet.

Electronic-Image Based Clearing (IBCS)

IBCS essentially requires conversion of the physical cheque in an electronic form and the converted electronic form of document moves forward as an authentic document for processing including fate decision making. NIFT has been working along with SBP and PBA member banks to prepare processes and guidelines regarding the IBCS. All stakeholders understand that it is now time to leapfrog into this direction and bring changes through technological innovation by replacing the old process of financial instrument movement and handling by implementing Image Based Clearing.

eCheque

NIFT eCheque is the digital version of conventional paper cheques for Banking Customers. eCheque is the digital issuance, lodgment and clearing of an electronic cheque through Bank’s digital channel that carries all the paper cheque requirements. On top of all existing offering of paper cheque, an eCheque offers enhanced convenience and security since it will be digitally signed by Payer bank. After a verification process, the echeque may be added in the Electronic-Image Based Clearing System (IBCS) cheque clearing cycle for quicker clearing and settlement.
