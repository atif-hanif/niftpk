Daniyal Alvi

CFO & CS

Daniyal Alvi is a Chartered Accountant with more than 14 years of experience specializing in Financial Control & Planning, Digital Transformation, and Business Intelligence & Analytics areas in Digital Financial Services, Startups, Telecommunication and Shared Service Centers entities. Prior to joining NIFT, he has been working in Telenor Bank (Easypaisa) where he headed the Business Planning & Analytics department supporting the management and the shareholders in business strategy & exponential growth as well as management of funding requirements with the shareholders and investment banks. During his association with Telenor Bank, he successfully organized the integration of Easypaisa Mobile Financial Services business with the legacy Banking organization after its acquisition by Telenor Group and lead the Organizational Transformation projects with the Board to uplift the legacy organization into a modern digital banking entity. Before that he has worked as Head of Finance for establishing various Digital Financial Services entities in South East Asia with support from Telenor Group. His initial assignment in Telenor had been to establish a captive Shared Service Center for Telenor Group for Finance and Technology functions serving more than 10 OpCos in Europe and Asia while leading the Financial Control function for Telenor Pakistan through Shared Services model. Daniyal has started his professional career from KPMG Islamabad office and has worked in KPMG Saudi Arabia and KPMG UAE for a few years in Audit and Transaction Advisory departments.
