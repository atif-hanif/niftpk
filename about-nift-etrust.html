About NIFTeTRUST

NIFTeTRUST is the most technologically advanced initiative of NIFT. We ensure the security and safety of commerce and communications over the internet.

NIFTeTRUST is the First Certificate Authority (CA) in Pakistan established under VeriSign as one of its Global Affiliates, with its data center certified to international standards X9.79 and ISO-27001. NIFTeTRUST has paved way for Pakistani organizations to safely and securely operate online business under a Global Trust hierarchy. It provides services which ensure security and safety of commerce and communications, addressing issues such as authentication, confidentiality or privacy, non-repudiation, and data integrity over the Internet.

NIFTeTRUST, now working under its indigenous build private CA hierarchy setup offers same features.
