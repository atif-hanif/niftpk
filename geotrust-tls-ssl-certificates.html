GeoTrust TLS/SSL Certificates

GEOTRUST TRUEBUSINESS ID EV

True BusinessID with EV certificate is GeoTrust’s premium business-class SSL security product, visually confirming the highest level of authentication available among SSL certificates.

  • Extended Validation SSL Certificates is the standard for trust.
  • Communicate encrypted and safe data transfer to customers.
  • Secures both www.example.com and example.com
  • Available in RSA and ECC algorithm.

Benefits included with True BusinessID with EV SSL/TLS certificates:

  • Basic validation & Basic support.
  • Site seal - DigiCert site seal is available by request.
  • Add up to 250 Subject Alternative Names (SANs) to your certificate.
  • Industry-leading warranties – True BusinessID with EV include a USD$1.5M Netsure Protection Warranty for your business.

GeoTrust TrueBusiness ID OV

True BusinessID certificates let your customers know that your site is trustworthy and that you take their security seriously enough to get your certificate from a globally trusted certificate authority.

  • Organization details in certificate information.
  • Communicate encrypted and safe data transfer to customers.
  • Secures both www.example.com and example.com.
  • Available in RSA and ECC algorithm.

Benefits included with True BusinessID SSL/TLS certificates:

  • Basic validation.
  • Site seal - DigiCert site seal is available by request.
  • Industry-leading warranties – True BusinessID include a USD$1.25M Netsure Protection Warranty for your business.
  • Add up to 250 Subject Alternative Names (SANs) to your certificate.
  • Wildcard and FQDN SANs (Unified Communications) – simply add up to 250 domain names and specify the number of Subject Alternative Name fields you need when you enroll for any True Business ID certificate.

GeoTrust DV SSL

GeoTrust DV SSL Certificates let you secure multiple domains (example.com) and wildcard domains (*.example.com) with one certificate. Ideal for cloud service providers and hosting companies.

  • Include a domain or wildcard domain in the common name field.
  • Add SANs to secure multiple domains and wildcard domains on one certificate (Adding SANs to a GeoTrust DV SSL certificate order may incur additional cost.).
  • Unlimited server license means you can install the certificate on multiple servers at no extra cost.
  • Trusted by all major browsers and operating systems.
  • Protect your website with RSA 2048+ encryption or ECC 256+ keys and SHA2-256 signature algorithm.
  • Meets and exceeds PCI Compliance requirements for TLS certificates.
